About



Hey, I'm Wandile!

As a Master's student in Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Dong Hwa University, I am immersed in a dynamic and innovative academic environment. My focus is on Big Data, AI, Data Science, and Machine Learning, leveraging advanced technologies to solve complex problems and drive impactful solutions. With a Bachelor's degree in the same field and an Associate Degree in Business Information Technology from Limkokwing University, I am well-equipped to tackle a variety of engineering challenges.

Prior to my current studies, I worked as an intern Network Administrator at the Ministry of Agriculture, Eswatini Government, where I gained hands-on experience in network management and system administration. Additionally, I served as a Teaching Assistant at National Dong Hwa University for two years, teaching courses in Linux, Big Data, and programming, which honed my skills in both education and technology.

When not engaged in academic or professional pursuits, I enjoy exploring the realms of artificial intelligence and data science, continuously learning and experimenting with new technologies. In my free time, I also like to unwind by playing video games, traveling, and enjoying photography. I have a passion for capturing the beauty of the world through my lens and sharing those moments with friends and family.

I've always been fascinated by technology and its potential to transform lives. My journey in computer science has been driven by a relentless curiosity and a desire to make a meaningful impact. I look forward to continuing my career in this exciting field, constantly pushing the boundaries of what is possible.

I am a confident, dedicated, and hardworking individual with a promising career in computer science, and I hope you find my website both informative and inspiring!
